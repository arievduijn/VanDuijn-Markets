Founder & Analyst
Arie E.F. van Duijn

Arie E.F. van Duijn is the founder of Van Duijn Markets and has over five years of experience in the financial markets, with a specialization in forex trading and technical analysis.

Over the years, he has developed a structured trading approach focused on liquidity, price inefficiencies, and recurring market behavior. His experience includes trading funded accounts for multiple proprietary trading firms, alongside actively managing personal capital.

Arie recognizes that the trading industry has increasingly been influenced by the culture surrounding social media, where unrealistic expectations and short-term results are often emphasized. His approach differs fundamentally by focusing on discipline, risk management, consistency, and a long-term perspective toward market participation.

This philosophy forms the foundation of Van Duijn Markets — a platform created to deliver professional market analysis and to promote a more structured, transparent, and sustainable approach to trading and financial markets.

About Us

Van Duijn Markets

Welcome to Van Duijn Markets — a modern financial analysis platform focused on the global forex markets.

Our objective is to provide clear, structured, and high-quality market analysis based on technical confluence, liquidity behavior, and evolving market conditions.

Our strategy is built on technical analysis, with liquidity, price inefficiencies, and market structure at the core of our approach. We analyze how price moves around key liquidity zones and identify where market imbalances develop in order to determine a clear and objective market bias. In addition, we focus on recurring market setups and repeatable price action patterns that historically provide high-probability trading opportunities.

The philosophy behind Van Duijn Markets is based on one fundamental principle: markets are constantly seeking balance. When price moves away from fair value, it creates movement toward liquidity and areas where the market aims to restore equilibrium.

Based on this perspective, we operate on both the long and short side of the market, allowing us to identify opportunities in both bullish and bearish market conditions.

We believe consistent performance is driven by discipline, risk management, and a deep understanding of market behavior. Van Duijn Markets provides a professional and analytical approach for traders seeking greater structure, clarity, and confidence in the markets.
